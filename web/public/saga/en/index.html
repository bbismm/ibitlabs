About this experiment

iBitLabs is a 0→N startup. The flagship public experiment is taking $1,000 to $10,000 via automated AI trading — but the goal isn't the money. It's building real skill in AI collaboration and AI entrepreneurship, in public, with every commit auditable.

If you build with AI agents — as a developer, a founder, an investor, or all three — this book is an 18-day record of one way to design the boundary between human judgment and AI observation.
