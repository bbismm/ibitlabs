**2/7**
There's a flag in the config file. It said `--paper`. Change it to `--live`, real money starts.

I changed it. One word.

And then I didn't press Enter.

I got up. Poured water. Came back and read the docs again — even though I already knew exactly what they said. I was just stalling. Buying ten more seconds.

---

**3/7**
Somewhere in those ten seconds, a sentence showed up in my head:

*"If this loses everything tonight, how do I tell anyone?"*

That thought had never crossed my mind during paper mode. Not once.

Real money comes in, and it comes in with it.

---

**4/7**
Eventually I pressed.

Nothing happened. The bot was way calmer than I was. It was waiting for a signal. I was waiting for it.

One hour. No trade.
Two hours. No trade.

I kept tabbing back to check the process was still alive. It was. It just wasn't doing anything.

---

**5/7**
By the third hour my eyes were getting sore from staring at a screen where nothing was changing.

So I went and washed dishes. Actual dishes. Not metaphorical.

Halfway through the sink, I noticed I'd relaxed. Something had shifted. I couldn't have told you what.

---

**6/7**
Day 1. Zero trades. Zero profit.

But most of what I learned all week came from those three hours where absolutely nothing happened.

The money hadn't moved — but I had.

---
