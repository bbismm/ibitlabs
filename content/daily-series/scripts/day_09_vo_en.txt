Day 9. For the first time, I didn't watch the charts. The bot made three trades. I checked once.

I can't code. 9 years in crypto. AI trades my thousand. Day 9.

That day I spent rewriting the website. Reframed every line from trading product to social experiment.

Meanwhile, the bot made three trades on its own. Net down about a dollar eighty.

I looked once. Then closed the tab.

Not because I didn't care. Because I'd finally built something I could stop policing.

The real test of any system you build is — can you stop watching it? If you can't, you didn't build a system. You built a second job. Most people can't tell the difference.

That was the first day I rented my attention back from the bot. It was cheaper than I expected.
