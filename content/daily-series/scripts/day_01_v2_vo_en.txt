Day 1 ended. Zero trades. Zero profit. And I learned more than 7 days of paper testing ever taught me.

I can't code. 9 years in crypto. 7 days ago I asked AI to build me a trading bot. I put a thousand down for this experiment — if it works, any ordinary person can use it.

This is Day 1 of AI trading my thousand dollars. Today it trades with my real money for the first time.

One word in the config — paper to live — real money starts. I changed it, and stared at the Enter key for 20 minutes.

Then this thought hits me — if this loses everything tonight, how do I tell anyone? That thought, in paper mode, never once crossed my mind. Not once.

I pressed. Nothing happened. One hour, no trade. Two hours, still nothing. It was waiting for a signal. I was waiting for it.

By hour three my eyes hurt. So I washed dishes. Halfway through the sink, I realized I'd relaxed. Something had shifted. I couldn't have told you what.

Day 1. Zero trades. But most of what I learned all week came from those three hours.
